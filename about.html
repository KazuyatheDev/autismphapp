About

Autism Directory for Families
across the Philippines.

Autismpinas is a free website directory built by an ausome parent for ausome families for the Pinoy community. I created this because I know how hard it is to find the right therapy center or SPED school for our kids. So I built the resource I wish I had. Autismpinas is my way of making that easier for every Filipino family going through the same journey.

This is a community effort. If you know a center, therapist, or school that's not listed yet, please add it. You'll be helping another parent who's searching right now.

Have a correction, suggestion, or want to reach out? Visit our Contact page .
