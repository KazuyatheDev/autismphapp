About

Supporting families
across the Philippines.

Autismph is a free created by ausowme dad, community-maintained directory that helps Filipino families find autism therapy centers, SPED schools, and support services close to home. All listings are reviewed by our team before they go live.

Navigating autism care in the Philippines can be overwhelming. There is no single place to find reliable, updated information about where to go for occupational therapy, speech therapy, ABA, or special education. Autismph exists to change that.

Our directory covers all major regions — from Metro Manila to Mindanao — and we are constantly working to expand our listings with the help of families, therapists, and center administrators.

Every center in this directory has been manually reviewed and approved before publication. We do not accept paid placements. Our goal is a trustworthy, complete resource for every Filipino family navigating autism care.

If you know of a center that is not listed, or if any information is out of date, please reach out through our contact page. We aim to review all submissions within 3 to 5 business days.
